Personal Project Plan - Vincitore Dental Clinic

Site Name: Vincitore Dental Clinic

This site is the website of a dental clinic called Vincitore Dental Clinic.

Site Purpose: for Vincitore Dental Clinic

This site provides an avenue for future and present patients to book appointments and see general information about the clinic's procedures and pricing including special offers. It will also provide a map for patients that do not know their way to the clinic

Scenarios: for Vincitore Dental Clinic

  • What are the clinic's operating days/hours?
  • How can I find my way to the clinic?
  • What procedures do they do and how much do they cost?
  • I would like to book an appointment online, how do I go about that?

Color schema

  • Secondary color: (#394b3e) for Heading text
  • Text color: (#222222) for paragraph and other text
  • Accent color: (#d72638) for buttons and to highlight important call to action information
